The Repair Service package takes a minimum of 4 hours for complete inspection, road-worthiness check, washing, billing and processing. Repairs and replacement if any can delay the time taken for delivery

Our service partners will submit an inspection report of your car. If any component is deemed defective / faulty, it will be brought to your notice and our partners will advice you on the best course of action.

Repair Car Service comprises of complete car health check-up followed by submission of an inspection report on the status of the vehicle. Post evaluation of the vehicle's condition, the service center will advise a course of action for the same. General service on the other hand comprises of oil filter replacement, oil change, suspension check-up, AC filter cleaning, fuel filter replacement. The cost incurred for the same would be significantly higher and would vary depending upon the make & model of the vehicle.
